Who We Are

Mission, Vision & Values

🎯

Our Mission

To spread smiles, education, and empowerment to every overlooked soul — transgender communities, underprivileged women, children with Down syndrome, the visually impaired, food-insecure families, and every person whose talents and heart deserve to be seen, loved, and supported.

👁️

Our Vision

A society where every person — regardless of gender identity, economic status, or background — has access to food, education, and equal opportunity.

💎

Our Values

Dignity for all. Transparency in action. Community-first thinking. Long-term empowerment over short-term relief. Compassion without judgment.

Shilpa Singh
Founder & Director
Meet Our Founder

Shilpa Singh

A social activist at heart, Shilpa Singh is the Founder & Director of Nikshi Foundation for Humanity and the Leader of Bangalore Navanirmana Party (BNP). Her journey spans engineering, counselling psychology, and social entrepreneurship — complemented by her passions for modeling, biking, and marathon running.

Our Journey

Milestones & Growth

FOUNDATION

Nikshi Foundation Established

Shilpa Singh founded the organisation in Bangalore with a focus on transgender empowerment and hunger relief in underserved communities.

HUNGER RELIEF

Daily Meal Distribution Launched

Started daily distribution of freshly prepared meals across multiple locations in Bangalore, reaching hundreds of food-insecure individuals every week.

EDUCATION

Education Initiative for Slum Children

Launched foundational literacy and numeracy classes for children in Bangalore's underserved neighbourhoods, alongside distribution of books and school supplies.

COVID RELIEF

COVID-19 Ration Drive (Phase 3)

Expanded operations to rural and peri-urban areas, distributing ration kits containing rice, lentils, oil, salt, and spices — each feeding a family of 4 for over a week.

TODAY

Growing Community Impact

Continuing to grow our reach across Bangalore, building community partnerships, and expanding our transgender empowerment and education programmes.
