● Active Project Gyaan

Education That
Goes to the Student

Gyaan — the Sanskrit word for knowledge — is the heart of this initiative. Project Gyaan brings structured learning directly to students at government schools in Bangalore who lack access to quality education resources.

Our volunteers teach core subjects, distribute school supplies, and build a nurturing environment where every child feels seen. No child is left behind because of where they were born.

✓
Government School Partnership
Working directly inside government schools in Bangalore.
✓
Core Subject Teaching
Literacy, numeracy, and science taught by trained volunteers.
✓
School Supplies Distribution
Books, notebooks, pens, and stationery provided at no cost.

● Active Nikshi Tech Talk Series

Bringing Tech
to the Next Generation

The Nikshi Tech Talk Series is an ongoing speaker programme that connects students at Bangalore University with leading professionals and innovators from the technology sector.

Each session brings a domain expert to campus — covering topics like AI, social entrepreneurship, product development, and careers in tech — opening doors for students who might otherwise never access these mentors.

📍 Venue
Bangalore University, Bangalore, Karnataka
🔄 Format
Ongoing series — new talks scheduled regularly throughout the year

● Active Community Outreach

Caring for Our
Elders

Every elder deserves dignity, warmth, and companionship. Nikshi Foundation regularly visits old age homes across Bangalore — spending meaningful time with residents, providing essentials, and reminding everyone that they are valued.

Most recently, we visited the Kamalamma Ramakrishnappa Old Age Home Trust in Bangalore — a visit that reminded us: humanity doesn't need a big budget. It needs time, presence, and care.

✓
Regular Visits
Consistent presence and companionship for residents.
✓
Essential Supplies
Providing daily essentials, snacks, and comfort items.
✓

Celebrating festivals and milestones with the elders.

● Active CSR Initiative

Corporate Social
Responsibility

Nikshi Foundation for Humanity is officially registered with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India to undertake CSR activities — enabling companies to channel their mandatory CSR contributions into meaningful, on-the-ground impact in Bangalore.

Under this initiative we organise community care visits, prepare and serve meals at old age homes and care institutions, and work alongside corporate partners to extend our reach. Every rupee donated under CSR goes directly to those who need it most.
